About Me:

My name is Matthew Pope, and I have loved theatre my entire life! I have made it my goal to learn a little but of everything- you'll see that reflected throughout this website, as I have many different areas of interest. I want to be the person that you can hire into your company and be able to help with any department.

I started my journey many years ago in small town Oregon, and have since been constantly moving up (both geographically and in my abilities). I graduated from Eastern Washington University in 2021 with my bachelor's in theatre and a minor in design. I've worked in a variety of enviornments from tutoring to fast food management, but my first passion has always been theatre and the arts. I hope I will be working with you soon!
